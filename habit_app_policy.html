Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (when you sign in with Google)
  • Habit Data: Your habits, completion dates, streaks, and statistics
  • Usage Data: App interactions and analytics to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Tiny Habits Tracker service
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send you motivational emails (if enabled)
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To process premium subscriptions

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Local Storage: Data stored on your device using Hive database
  • Cloud Storage: Data synced to Firebase Firestore (when signed in)
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Firebase: For authentication and cloud storage
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • SendGrid: For sending motivational emails (if enabled)
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of email notifications
  • Export your data

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, email us at: support@tinyhabits.app
