1. Introduction

Welcome to CardSnap Business Card Scanner ("CardSnap", "we", "our", or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains how CardSnap handles your information when you use our mobile application.

CardSnap is committed to protecting your privacy. Unlike many other apps, we have designed CardSnap to work completely offline and to keep all your data on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

CardSnap does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Business card data or images
  • Contact information extracted from cards
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage Only

All data in CardSnap is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Business Card Images: Stored in your device's local storage
  • Contact Information: Saved in a local database (Hive) on your device
  • App Settings: Stored locally in app preferences

3.2 No Cloud Storage

CardSnap does not use any cloud storage services. Your data never leaves your device. We do not have access to your information at any time.

3.3 Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual business cards at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • No data remains on any external servers (because we don't use any)

4. Permissions Explained

CardSnap requests the following permissions, which are used only for the stated purposes:

4.1 Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To take photos of business cards
  • Usage: Only activated when you tap the scan button
  • Data: Photos are processed locally and never transmitted

4.2 Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save business card images on your device and to select images from your gallery
  • Usage: Only used to read/write files in the app's designated storage area
  • Data: All files remain on your device

4.3 No Internet Permission

CardSnap's core functionality does not require internet access. The app works 100% offline.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OCR Technology

CardSnap uses Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition:

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to Google servers
  • ML Kit runs entirely offline after initial download

5.2 No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crashlytics
  • Or any other tracking tools

5.3 No Advertising

CardSnap contains no advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

CardSnap does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Data Security

While we don't collect data, we take security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally using secure storage methods
  • Images are compressed and stored in app-private directories
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • Your device's built-in security protects your data

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit any business card information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual cards or all data by uninstalling
  • Export: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
