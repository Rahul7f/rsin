Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Stranger Chat (“the App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. The App is intended strictly for adults 18 years and older.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Anonymous Usage

The App allows users to chat anonymously. We do not require your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

We may collect the following non-personal data for functionality and analytics:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash logs and performance data
  • Approximate location (country only)
  • App usage statistics

1.3 Chat Messages

Chats are stored temporarily using Firebase Firestore for real-time functionality. Messages may be processed automatically for moderation purposes (e.g., inappropriate content detection). We do not sell or share chat content with third parties.

1.4 Reports & Safety Logs

When a user reports another user, the report details are stored for safety review and enforcement actions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable anonymous chat matching
  • To detect and prevent harmful or illegal content
  • To enforce app policies and bans
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To manage subscriptions and premium features

3. Child Safety & CSAM Zero Tolerance

The App is strictly for users aged 18+ only. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any content involving minors in harmful or sexual contexts results in:

  • Immediate & permanent account ban
  • Content removal
  • Reporting to relevant authorities

Our full Child Safety Policy can be found here:
Child Safety & Anti-CSAM Policy

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication (anonymous login)
  • Real-time chat
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting

4.2 AdMob

We display ads to support the service. AdMob may collect anonymous advertising identifiers and device data.

4.3 Google Play Billing

We use Google Play Billing for premium subscriptions. We do not access or store your payment details.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security to protect your data. However, no online service can guarantee 100% security.

6. Data Retention

  • Chat messages: temporary (deleted after chat ends or after defined retention period)
  • Reports & bans: retained for safety enforcement
  • Analytics data: retained as per Firebase policies

7. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Deletion of safety reports related to your account
  • Blocking or removal of harmful content

Contact us for such requests.

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
