Privacy Policy for Stranger Chat

Developer: RSIN
Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application “Stranger Chat” (the “App”), published by developer RSIN, collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is intended strictly for adults 18 years and older.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Anonymous Usage

Stranger Chat allows users to chat anonymously. We do not require your name, email, phone number, or personally identifiable information.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device model & OS version
  • Crash logs & diagnostics
  • Approximate location (country only)
  • Usage analytics

1.3 Chat Messages

Chat messages are stored temporarily in Firebase Firestore for real-time delivery. Messages may be automatically scanned for policy violations (e.g., harmful content, CSAE). We do not store chat history permanently and do not sell or share chat content with third parties.

1.4 Reports & Safety Logs

User reports are stored to help enforce safety rules, investigate abuse, and ban violators.

2. How We Use Collected Information

  • Enable anonymous chatting and real-time matching
  • Detect and prevent harmful or illegal behavior
  • Moderate content and enforce bans
  • Improve app performance, safety, and user experience
  • Support premium subscriptions

3. Child Safety & CSAE Zero-Tolerance Policy

Stranger Chat is strictly for adults 18+ only. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (“CSAE”), including but not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Child grooming
  • Sexualized chat involving or directed toward minors
  • Sharing or requesting images of minors
  • Predatory or harmful behavior toward minors

If any CSAE is detected:

  • Content is removed immediately
  • User is permanently banned
  • We report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or relevant law enforcement, as required by law

Stranger Chat provides an in-app Report User feature to quickly report harmful or illegal content. Reports are reviewed and acted upon swiftly as part of our moderation process.
