Child Safety & Anti-CSAM Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This policy outlines our strict commitment to preventing Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and protecting minors from exploitation within our app. Our app is intended only for adults aged 18 and above. We take child safety extremely seriously and comply with all international and regional laws regarding CSAM prevention.

1. Age Restriction

Our platform is strictly for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly allow minors to use our app. We prohibit:

  • Creating an account or accessing the app under the age of 18
  • Depictions of minors in sexual or harmful contexts
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any interaction targeting minors

2. Zero-Tolerance for CSAM

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any content involving minors in a sexual, exploitative, or abusive context is prohibited. Violations result in:

  • Immediate and permanent account ban
  • Content removal
  • Mandatory reporting to relevant law enforcement authorities

3. Moderation & Prevention Practices

To keep our community safe, we enforce:

  • Automatic and manual content review
  • Community guidelines prohibiting sexual or harmful content
  • Instant removal of illegal content
  • Permanent banning of accounts violating child safety rules

4. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report inappropriate content, harmful behavior, or suspected CSAM directly inside the app. Additionally, child safety issues may be reported to:

Email: rsin7f@gmail.com

We respond to child safety reports with the highest priority.

5. Cooperation With Authorities

We comply with all applicable local, regional, and national regulations regarding child safety. If CSAM is detected, we:

  • Preserve necessary data as required by law
  • Report the incident to appropriate law enforcement agencies
  • Assist investigations in accordance with legal obligations

6. Commitment to a Safe Community

We are dedicated to creating a platform where users feel safe and protected. Any violation of this policy is taken extremely seriously.
